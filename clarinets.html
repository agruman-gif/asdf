The clarinet is the third highest pitched instrument in the woodwind section, and has the largest pitch range in the woodwind section.

While this instrument may look similar to the oboe at first, its timbre and range contrast greatly with the oboe.

The clarinet has a mellow sound, and a large auxiliary family. This includes 8 additional clarinets!

In an orchestral setting, only a few of these auxiliaries are used regularly.




Auxiliaries in the clarinet family, from highest in pitch to lowest.
  1. Ab clarinet
  2. Eb clarinet
  3. A clarinet
  4. Alto clarinet
  5. Basset horn
  6. Bass clarinet
  7. Eb Contra-Alto clarinet
  8. Bb Contra-Bass clarinet
